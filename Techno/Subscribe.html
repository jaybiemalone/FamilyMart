Who is Medicine Management Program for?



1.Busy moms who juggle work, household chores, groceries and school meetings. With Medicine Management Program, buying their family’s regular supply of medicines and supplements will be ticked off her list as supplies can be delivered regularly.

2.Those who take maintenance medicines. Avoid missing your medicines by subscribing. With this, you’ll be assured that you have enough stocks at home.

3.Young professionals who are busy with their career to become financially secured while also enjoying their social life. Forget the task of dropping by to a nearby store or placing the order online again and again. Subscribe to the program, sit back and wait for your delivery.

4.OFWs who sends money to their relatives regularly for medicines. Spare yourselves the trouble of sending money. Subscribe and we will take care of your family by making sure that they get their supplies on time.

How do I subscribe to this program?


1.Fill out the form with your details.
2.Upload your prescription, if applicable.
3.Upload your Senior Citizen documents, if applicable.
4.Click the Subscribe button.
5.Our Customer Service Representative will contact you for verification.
6.Wait for the delivery.

How do I pay for my subscription?

You will not be required to pay the whole duration of your preferred subscription. Payment will be done every delivery and you may pay via bank transfer, GCash and online credit card.

For bank transfer, our Customer Service Representative will send you the details at least 2 days before your scheduled delivery. You must send a proof of payment via email or Viber at least 1 day before your scheduled delivery.

For online credit card and GCash payment, an invoice will be sent to you via email. A link will be provided so you can easily pay via credit card.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscription can be cancelled anytime. Just send us an email request at onlinesales@FamilyMart.com.ph at least 3 days before your scheduled delivery.
